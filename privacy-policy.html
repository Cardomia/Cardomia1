Privacy Policy

cardomia.com

Last Updated: January 2025

Important: This Privacy Policy complies with GDPR (EU) and US privacy regulations. We are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to cardomia.com ("we," "us," or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we collect and use your email address when you subscribe to our mailing list.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information from you:

  • Email Address: When you subscribe to our launch notification list
  • Technical Data: Browser type and device information (automatically collected)
  • Timestamp: Date and time of subscription

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your email address solely for the following purpose:

  • To notify you when cardomia.com launches
  • To send you updates about our platform development (optional)

We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, we process your email address based on:

  • Consent: You have given clear consent by submitting your email address to receive launch notifications

5. Data Storage

Your email address is securely stored in our database (Supabase) with industry-standard encryption. We retain your email until:

  • You request deletion
  • You unsubscribe from our mailing list
  • Two years after our platform launch (if you haven't engaged with our emails)

6. Your Rights (GDPR & US Privacy Laws)

EU Users' Rights (GDPR):

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your email address we have stored
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of your email address
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your email address at any time
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Unsubscribe at any time

US Users' Rights (CCPA/CPRA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to deletion of your email address
  • Right to opt-out (unsubscribe)

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your email address, including encryption and secure database access controls.

8. International Data Transfers

Your email address may be stored on servers located outside your country. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with GDPR and other applicable laws.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect email addresses from children under 16.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at:

  • Email: info@cardomia.com
